Progress Quest Slog: Chinbreak Island

This game plays itself, so you don't have to, as controlled by a strategy you select, or create.

Use "New Game" to start a new game, or read more about it all.

 

by Grumdrig · version 1.alpha

Life on Chinbreak Island had been generally peaceful for as long as anyone there could remember, with Hardwarf and Eff, Dunkling and Human, living in relative harmony. But in the year 526, dangerous monsters of all different sorts began to appear around the island, causing no head of mayhem to travellers and homesteaders alike.

Some supposed that this alarming change could be related to the goings on observed at nearby Sygnon Isle, where odd plumes, eerie noises, and flashes of light were all noted by those who dwelled on the coast opposite, along with noxious odors carried on winds from that direction. The fact is that such speculation was correct. An evil presence had taken up residence high on Mt Emkell on Sygnon Isle, and was practicing such magics as to cook up trouble on Chinbreak.

Unfortunately, time was very limited for the Chinbreakers. As the evil one's power grew, so did the suffering of the once happy people.

But one hero, little more than a child, would prove to be the only hope for the dwellers on Chinbreak. Would this person be able to build their power, training and studying, fighting and upgrading, enough to defeat this threat? That's up to you to prove!
